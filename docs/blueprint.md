# **App Name**: StockMaster

## Core Features:

- Product Management: Create and update product details including name, SKU, category, and unit of measure.
- Receipts Management: Record incoming stock from vendors and automatically update stock levels upon validation.
- Delivery Orders: Manage outgoing stock for customer shipments, decrementing stock levels upon validation.
- Internal Transfers: Track stock movements between different locations within the company.
- Stock Adjustments: Adjust stock levels based on physical counts, logging all adjustments for audit trails.
- Dashboard KPIs: Display key performance indicators such as total products in stock, low stock items, pending receipts, and deliveries.
- Low Stock Alerts: Generative AI tool which proactively identify items falling below reorder points, triggering alerts based on historical data analysis.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#00BFFF), embodying trust, efficiency, and clarity in inventory management.
- Background color: Light gray (#D3D3D3), providing a neutral and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Sea green (#2E8B57), signaling growth and completion.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use minimalist, easily recognizable icons for each inventory action (receipts, deliveries, adjustments) and product categories.
- A clean, modular layout, inspired by leading digital creative agencies, with clear separation of sections and consistent use of whitespace.
- Subtle transitions and animations to indicate stock level changes and operation validations, providing visual feedback to user actions.
